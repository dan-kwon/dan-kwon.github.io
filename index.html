Hi, I'm Dan

This is my personal website where I share notes on AI, machine learning, and whatever I'm learning. Currently, I am a Machine Learning Engineer at Headspace, where I build and deploy conversational agents and safety systems for digital mental health applications. My work centers on AI safety and building agentic conversational workflows in the form of Ebb, Headspace's empathetic AI companion.

I’m particularly interested in understanding model behavior at a granular level via mechanistic interpretability, translating those insights into robust, reliable systems that perform well in real-world environments, and designing safeguards that scale with increasingly capable models.

All views are my own, though mechanistic analysis would likely reveal they’re mostly linear combinations of things I’ve read.

Outside of work, I enjoy traveling, photography, and drinking coffee in diners.
